### **Slide 2: The Problem - Urban Gridlock**
*   **Economic Loss:** Traffic congestion costs Indian urban centers billions in productivity.
*   **Environment:** Vehicular idling at red lights is a primary source of urban CO2 emissions.
*   **Energy Security:** High fuel imports are exacerbated by inefficient driving patterns in urban "stop-and-go" traffic.
*   **The "Pre-Timed" Barrier:** Conventional traffic lights don't adapt to real-time vehicle density.

---

### **Slide 3: Our Solution - GLOSA Implementation**
*   **What is GLOSA?** Green Light Optimal Speed Advisory.
*   **Core Concept:** An AI system that calculates and communicates the "ideal speed" to a driver so they arrive at the intersection exactly when the light turns green.
*   **Goal:** Achieve a "Green Wave" across city corridors, eliminating unnecessary stops.

---

### **Slide 4: System Architecture (High-Level)**
*   **The Perception Layer (AI):** Uses YOLO/OpenCV to analyze live camera feeds and count vehicle queues at junctions.
*   **The Brain (Backend):** A Node.js server that integrates the AI data to calculate optimal speeds for approaching vehicles.
*   **The Interface (Frontend):** A real-time React dashboard providing live telemetry map views and the "Driver Advisory Feed."
*   **The Protocol (V2I):** Vehicle-to-Infrastructure synchronization ensuring sub-second latency for speed advisories.

---

### **Slide 5: Technology Stack**
*   **AI/ML:** Python, YOLO (Object Detection), OpenCV (Queue Analysis).
*   **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js (High-concurrency API orchestration).
*   **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion (Real-time dynamic UI).
*   **Maps & Geospatial:** React-Leaflet, OpenStreetMap API.
*   **Styling:** Modern "Control Room" Dark Mode aesthetic for low-distraction environments.

---

### **Slide 6: Innovation for Indian Roads**
*   **Indigenous Resilience:** Unlike Western systems, our AI is trained for Indian traffic patterns (heterogeneous vehicles and unlane-led behavior).
*   **Scalability:** Low-cost edge computing implementation suitable for Tier-2 and Tier-3 Indian cities.
*   **Atmanirbhar Vision:** A 100% indigenous software stack, reducing dependence on expensive foreign traffic management propriety software.
